Who We Are

Built in Houston. Reaching the World.

Every child deserves access to chess, Coding, STEM, and the opportunities they unlock — regardless of zip code, income, or geography.

StemToSuccess began as a club during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, founded by Ronak Hiwale and Riona Hiwale with a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. As the initiative grew, it evolved into a nonprofit organization, empowering students in the Greater Houston Area through in-person programs and reaching learners worldwide through virtual and remote education.

Driven by a passion for education and community impact, our dedicated volunteers, coaches, and mentors donate their time to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed — from Houston's neighborhoods to communities across the globe through virtual learning.

Help StemToSuccess Bring Free Chess & STEM Education to Every Child

Every child deserves a chance to think strategically, code confidently, and dream big — but not every child gets that chance.

StemToSuccess is a Houston-based nonprofit that provides completely free chess coaching, STEM tutoring, coding mentorship, and virtual learning programs to students in the Greater Houston Area and beyond. Since 2020, we've reached 200+ students through programs at Houston Public Library, West University Recreation Center, and our annual Global Guru Online Chess BootCamp — serving learners worldwide.

We don't charge a single dollar to students or families. Every program, every chess set, every coaching session is funded by generous people like you.

We are run entirely by volunteers who believe talent is everywhere — but opportunity is not. Help us change that. Donate today, share our story, or sign up to volunteer.
