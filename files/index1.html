Who We Are

BUILT BY BELIEVERS, FOR ACHIEVERS

Every child deserves access to the future that STEM can unlock — regardless of zip code or income.

STEM to Success was founded with a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. We're here to change that — one student, one mentor, one breakthrough at a time.


As a registered nonprofit, 100% of your donations go directly toward student programs, scholarships, and resources that create measurable difference in young lives across Houston and beyond.
