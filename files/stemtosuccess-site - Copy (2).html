Who We Are

BUILT IN HOUSTON. REACHING THE WORLD.

Every child deserves access to chess, STEM, and the future it can unlock — regardless of zip code, income, or geography.

StemToSuccess was founded in 2021 by Ronak Hiwale and Riona Hiwale with a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. Based in Houston, Texas, we serve the Greater Houston Area through in-person programs — and reach students across the globe through virtual and remote learning.


As a nonprofit organization, we are driven entirely by a passion for education and community impact. Our volunteers, coaches, and mentors donate their time to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed — from Houston to the world.
